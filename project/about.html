Discover the Beauty of Ethiopia

Welcome to Abisinya Tour and travels, your premier travel partner for exploring the wonders of Ethiopia. Establshed in 2024, our mission is to offer unforgettable experience that immerse you in the rich culture, stunning landscapes, and historical treasures of Ethiopia.
At Ethiopia Tours, we are passionate about showcasing the unique beauty of our country. From the majestic Simien Mountains to the ancient rock-hewn churches of Lalibela, we provide expertly crafted tour packages that cater to adventurers, history buffs, and cultural enthusiasts alike. Our team of experienced guides and travel experts are dedicated to ensuring your journey is both enriching and seamless.

History

Ethiopia Tours has swiftly become a standout in the travel and tourism industry. What began as a small group of dedicated explorers with a deep love for Ethiopia’s diverse landscapes and rich heritage has evolved into a flourishing enterprise, drawing travelers from across the globe. From our modest beginnings to becoming a trusted name in Ethiopian tourism, our journey has been as remarkable as the destinations we showcase.
Our story began in 2010 when a group of passionate travel enthusiasts came together with a shared vision: to reveal the hidden gems of Ethiopia to the world. We started with just a handful of tours, focusing on the breathtaking Simien Mountains and the historic sites of Lalibela. These initial tours were met with overwhelming enthusiasm, encouraging us to expand our offerings and explore new regions.
By 2013, Ethiopia Tours had grown significantly, adding several new destinations to our roster, including the lush Omo Valley, the ancient city of Axum, and the vibrant capital, Addis Ababa. Our commitment to providing unique and authentic experiences set us apart, and word of mouth quickly spread, attracting more adventurous souls seeking to uncover the wonders of Ethiopia.
In 2015, we launched our cultural immersion tours, designed to offer travelers deeper insights into the traditions and daily lives of Ethiopia’s diverse communities. These tours, which included homestays and participatory activities, became immensely popular, highlighting our dedication to sustainable and responsible tourism. We believe that genuine connections with local communities enrich the travel experience and foster mutual respect and understanding.

As our reputation grew, so did our team. We welcomed experienced guides, knowledgeable historians, and local experts who shared our passion for Ethiopian culture and history. Their expertise and enthusiasm have been instrumental in crafting immersive and informative tours that leave a lasting impression on our guests. Our team’s dedication to excellence has been a cornerstone of our success.
In 2018, we achieved a significant milestone by partnering with several international travel agencies, expanding our reach and making it easier for travelers from around the world to discover Ethiopia. This collaboration allowed us to offer more comprehensive packages, including flights, accommodations, and guided tours, ensuring a seamless travel experience for our clients. Our growth has never compromised our core values of quality and authenticity.
Ethiopia Tours has always been at the forefront of promoting responsible tourism. We prioritize environmental conservation and work closely with local communities to support sustainable practices. Our tours are designed to minimize the ecological footprint while maximizing positive impacts on the regions we visit. We are proud of our initiatives that contribute to the preservation of Ethiopia’s natural and cultural heritage.
Looking ahead, we are excited to continue our journey of discovery and growth. We remain committed to providing exceptional travel experiences that celebrate the beauty and diversity of Ethiopia. With our expanding range of tours, dedicated team, and unwavering commitment to responsible tourism, Ethiopia Tours is poised for many more years of adventure and exploration. We invite you to join us and create memories that will last a lifetime.
